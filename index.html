Clustering in Dynamic Networks Under Noise

In collaboration with Dr. Weston Viles

Our research involves analyzing brain network activity data, aiming to enhance the understanding of brain network during epileptic seizures. We aim to analyse the time taken for the brain seizure to turn from detection to symptom, i.e., understanding the nature of the giant connected component in the brain by using explosive percolation with clique noise.
